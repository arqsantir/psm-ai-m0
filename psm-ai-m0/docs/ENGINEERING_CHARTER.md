# Engineering Charter

## North Star
Every engineering decision must answer one question:

> Will this make the three-minute demo clearer, faster, or more memorable?

If not, defer it to `BACKLOG.md`.

## Frozen rules
- Ship a functional demo before expanding the platform.
- Bob is an AI territory inspector, not a generic chatbot.
- The interface must make users feel the land is speaking through Bob.
- The Colina Condesa case is the canonical cached demo.
- Keep the MVP to five modules: Upload, Bob Analysis, Territory Dashboard, Opportunity Score, Generate PSM Report.
- Prefer deterministic demo behavior over fragile complexity.
