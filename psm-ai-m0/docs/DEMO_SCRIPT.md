**0:00–0:25 — Problem**  
Territory information is fragmented across consultants. Nobody reads the whole system.

**0:25–0:50 — Bob**  
Upload a survey. Bob reads the territory and lets the land speak.

**0:50–1:40 — Colina Condesa**  
Show the masterplan and reveal water, slope, vegetation, access, and development signals.

**1:40–2:20 — Opportunity Score**  
Explain why the best-fit development is low-density, wellness-led, and hydrologically responsive.

**2:20–2:50 — Report**  
Generate the PSM Report as a clear decision document.

**2:50–3:00 — Closing**  
We don't design buildings. We design systems that become places.
